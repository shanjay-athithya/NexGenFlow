About Our Website

Welcome to our website! Here's some information about how it works:

  • Functionality: Our website is designed to manage water flow networks. Users can create and visualize networks, optimize flow, and perform various network management tasks.
  • Interacting with the Website: Users can create nodes and edges to represent points and connections in the network. They can also perform optimization tasks to find the most efficient flow paths.
  • Conditions for Edges: Edges represent connections between nodes in the network. They must have a valid source and target node, as well as a flow rate indicating the amount of water that can flow through them.The tank node cannot be selected as a target as it is the source. The outlet Type node cannot be selected as a source as it is the end point of the water network ie.taps. The valve Node acts as connecting internal nodes in the network.
  • Optimization: Our website offers optimization algorithms such as Edmonds-Karp, Ford-Fulkerson, Dinic, and Push-Relabel to find the maximum flow in the network. Users can visualize the optimized flow paths to understand the network's efficiency.
